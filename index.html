Short biography

Devis Bianchini has been assistant professor since 2005 at the Engineering Faculty of the University of Brescia. From 2002 to 2005 he carried out research and teaching activities as a PhD student in the field of database and Information System technologies. He received a Master Degree in Electronics Engineering in 2002 and a PhD in Information Engineering in 2005 at the University of Brescia.
